Introduction

What is an Inventory & Stock Management System?

Inventory and Stock Management System is a software solution that helps businesses track, organize, and manage their inventory efficiently. It records stock levels, monitors product movement, and helps prevent overstocking or stock shortages. By automating inventory processes, the system improves accuracy, saves time, and supports better business decisions. It is widely used in retail stores, warehouses, supermarkets, and manufacturing industries.

About the Project

One dashboard. Total control.

StockPro is a web-based inventory management system developed to simplify how businesses handle their stock. It allows users to add products, set reorder points, get low stock alerts and manage everything from one clean dashboard.

Main Purpose

Why StockPro exists.

The main purpose of an Inventory and Stock Management System is to efficiently manage and monitor the availability of products in a business. It helps keep accurate records of stock, prevents shortages and overstocking, reduces manual errors, and improves overall operational efficiency. The system also supports better decision-making by providing real-time inventory information, saving both time and costs.

How It Works

From setup to full control in minutes.

STEP 01

Add Your Products

Enter product name, SKU, category, price and stock quantity. SKUs (Stock Keeping Units) are unique codes that identify each product — useful when items come in variants like size or colour. Categories group your products (e.g. Electronics, Clothing, Food) so the dashboard stays organized as your inventory grows.

Product Name SKU Category Price Quantity
STEP 02

Set Reorder Points

Define the minimum stock level for each product. A reorder point is the quantity at which StockPro should warn you to restock — for example, setting a reorder point of 10 means once stock drops to 10 units, an alert fires automatically. Different products need different thresholds: fast-selling items need higher reorder points, slow-moving stock can have lower ones.

Per-Product Threshold Custom Minimums Auto Alert Trigger
STEP 03

Monitor the Dashboard

Once products are added, your dashboard gives you a live overview of your entire inventory — quantities, categories, alert statuses and product details all in one place. You can see at a glance which products are healthy, which are running low and which need immediate action.

STEP 04

Receive Low Stock Alerts

When a product's quantity drops to or below its reorder point, StockPro flags it automatically in the Low Stock Alerts section. No manual checking needed — the system watches every product continuously and surfaces only the ones that need your attention.

STEP 05

Search & Filter Your Inventory

Use the search bar to find a product by name or SKU in seconds. Use category filters to view an entire group at once. This is especially useful as your inventory grows — instead of scrolling through everything, you narrow down to exactly what you need instantly.

STEP 06

Restock Products

When a product is running low, open it from the dashboard and update the stock quantity. The quantity reflects immediately across the system, the alert clears once the product is back above its reorder point and your dashboard returns to a healthy state — no page refresh needed.

STEP 07

Edit or Update Product Details

Products don't stay the same forever. Prices change, categories get reorganized, SKUs get updated. StockPro lets you edit any product's details at any time directly from the dashboard without having to delete and re-add it.

STEP 08

Keep Inventory Accurate Over Time

As stock moves — through sales, deliveries or manual corrections — you update quantities in StockPro. Every change reflects immediately, giving you and your team a live and accurate picture of stock health at all times. Accurate data means better purchasing decisions, fewer emergency orders and no unexpected stockouts.

FAQ

Common questions.

What happens when stock hits zero?

If a product's quantity reaches zero, it gets marked as Out of Stock on the dashboard. It will also appear in the alerts section so you can restock it immediately.

Can I delete a product?

Yes. Any product can be removed from the dashboard at any time. Once deleted, it will no longer appear in your inventory or trigger any alerts.

Can I edit a product after adding it?

Absolutely. You can update any product's name, SKU, category, price, quantity or reorder point at any time directly from the dashboard.

Why StockPro Over a Spreadsheet?

Spreadsheets break down fast when inventory gets complex. StockPro gives you automatic alerts, real-time updates and a clean dashboard built specifically for stock management — no formulas, no manual tracking, no errors.
